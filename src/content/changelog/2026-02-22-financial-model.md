Built a full financial model by pulling 9 months of bank transactions through a [Copilot Money CLI](https://github.com/JaviSoto/copilot-money-cli) into the vault. Income, burn rate, assets, debt, equity, and exit scenarios — all in natural language queryable files. The system now has the same visibility into personal finances as it does into projects and meetings.
